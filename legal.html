Server Rules & Policies

By accessing AquixMC, you agree to comply with all server rules. Failure to follow these rules may result in disciplinary action.

General Information

  • Players must be at least 13 years old to play on the server
  • Staff members reserve the right to modify these rules without prior notice
  • All Mojang/Microsoft EULA and ToS apply in addition to our rules

Enforcement Policy

  • Penalties are applied at staff discretion based on offense severity
  • Repeat offenses will result in escalated punishments
  • All punishments are final unless appealed properly

Server Rules

Severe Offenses

  • Hacking/Cheating: Using any unauthorized mods, clients, or exploits
  • DDoS/DoS Threats: Threatening or attempting to attack server infrastructure
  • Extreme Toxicity: Racial slurs, death threats, or extreme harassment
Penalty: Permanent ban (no appeals for extreme cases)

High Severity Offenses

  • Account Sharing: Letting others play on your account
  • Scamming: Attempting to steal accounts, items, or money
  • Bug Exploitation: Abusing game-breaking bugs for unfair advantage
Penalty: 14–30 day ban + wipe (repeat: permanent ban)

Medium Severity Offenses

  • Toxicity: Excessive harassment, bullying, or hate speech
  • Spamming: Excessive chat/command spam or advertising
  • Team Griefing: Intentionally sabotaging teammates in games
Penalty: 1–7 day mute/ban (repeat: 14–30 days)

Low Severity Offenses

  • Minor Toxicity: Mild insults or trash talk (context dependent)
  • Minor Spam: Repeating messages or excessive caps
  • Staff Disrespect: Arguing with staff about valid punishments
Penalty: Warning or 1–24 hour mute (repeat: 1–7 days)

Ban Appeal Process

How to Appeal

  1. Join our Discord server
  2. Navigate to #ticket-support channel
  3. Use the provided template
  4. Wait for staff response (typically 1-3 days)

What Not to Do

  • Don't create multiple appeals for the same ban
  • Don't harass staff about your appeal status
  • Don't lie in your appeal - we check evidence
